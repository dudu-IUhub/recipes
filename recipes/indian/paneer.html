Panneer

Description

This easy paneer recipe is great for vegetarian. You can add paneer to your favorite curry or dish.

Homemade paneer is softer and lighter than the tofu-like consistency of paneer found in may Indian restaurants

Ingredients

  • 1 gallon milk
  • 1 quart buttermilk
  • 1 cup canola oil for frying

Steps

  1. Pour milk into a large pot and bring to a boil over medium heat; watch carefully to prevent milk from boiling over.
  2. Line a colander with cheesecloth. Pour milk mixture sit in the conlander until curds are settled and dripping stops, about 2 hours
  3. Transfer strained curds to a food processor. Process until smooth and mixture can be formed intoa ball.
  4. Turn cheese out onto a clean surface and knead until smooth. Form inta a ball and wrap in plastic. Refrigerae until needed.
