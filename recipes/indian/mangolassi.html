Mango Lassi

Description

Mango lassi is very rich and luxurious dring drink with yogurt an dmangoes.

It is the perfect summer drink when served chilled

Ingredients

  • 1 half cups mangoes - peeled, seeded, chopped, and chilled
  • 1 half cups plain yogurt
  • 1 half cup cold milk
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons confectioners'sugar
  • 1 half teaspoon ground cardamom

Steps

  1. Combine mangos, yogurt, milk, cream, confectioners' sugar, and cardamom in a blender, blend until smooth and forthy.
